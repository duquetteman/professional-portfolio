Never Stop Learning

I am enrolled in the UT coding bootcamp and hope to find gainful employment in the Austin area after graduation. I would like to start in web developmemnt and eventually explore machine learning and data science. I am fascinated by the technologies that make our modern world work.

My education and professional experiences cover a broad spectrum. I just earned my CAPM - Certified Associate of Project Management Certification through PMI. I have a BS in Psychology from Iowa State University, and an MBA with a Finance emphasis from Ashford Univeristy. I have worked for Apple for the last 9 years as a Senior Technical Advisor and Team Manager Support. Prior to that, I was a Realtor and started my own Property Management company. I have also worked with AT&T in a role that focused primarily on customer support and sales, with team management duties assigned as needed.

I live near Austin, Texas, with my wife and 6 beautiful children. I enjoy spending time with them and watching them grow. We watch movies, take walks, and cook together. Family time is one of life's little pleasures.
